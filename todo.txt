state
	stocks: an array of objects which contain
		stock name
		quantity the person has

	funds: an integer representing the users pocket money

	market

		each company has a name and a price/stock

	buy: a certian quantity of shares from a certain company.
	sell: a certain quantity of shares from a certain company.
